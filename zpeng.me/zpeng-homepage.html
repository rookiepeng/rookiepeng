About Me

Dr. Zhengyu Peng is a radar systems engineer with deep expertise in automotive radar, signal processing, and antenna arrays. He earned his Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from Texas Tech University in 2018, following graduate work at Zhejiang University in China.

At Aptiv, he leads work on next-generation high-resolution imaging radar and automotive interference mitigation. He currently serves as Associate Editor for IEEE Transactions on Radar Systems and previously held the same role for IEEE TIM. He sits on the SPIE Defense+Commercial Sensing Radar Sensor Technology Committee.

Beyond engineering, Zhengyu is an avid maker — 3D printing robots, building hexapods, and releasing open-source tools for the radar and RF engineering community.
